# Buffs Timer
### This mod was made by a request of my friend and i decided to publish it, maybe someone finds it useful
## What is it
This mod shows how long your consumable item (injector, bar, p-beans, grub, etc.) will last and what bonuses it gives you (along with their strength) in format:

BuffName BuffAmount (MaxBuffAmount) 

![](docs/demo.gif)

Also its supports a Metabolic Stasis perk that increases the duration of consumable items.
